Why Cadillac should stop making small SUVs1

Cadillac is recognized as America's premier brand, cherished for its luxury. It's famous for being large and impressive. And when you hear 'Cadillac,' you probably picture the stylish Escalade SUV. For every luxury brand, pinpointing the target market and recognizing direct competitors with similar luxurious offerings holds great significance. Moreover, within the realm of luxury, the term inherently signifies exclusivity.

Considering the above factors, I have 4 reasons why Cadillac show stop making small SUVs and instead concentrate solely on larger SUVs and sedans.
